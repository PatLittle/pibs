## Our commitment to transparency

Canada Post is subject to the *Access to Information Act* and the *Privacy Act*. We're committed to meeting our disclosure obligations under this legislation. We respect your right of access and balance that with protecting commercially sensitive, corporate, and third-party information.

## Information you can request

Info Source is a publication containing information about all Government of Canada departments, its organization, and information holdings. This includes those of Crown corporations such as Canada Post. Info Source helps members of the public to exercise their rights under the [*Access to Information Act*](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/A-1/%20) and the federal [*Privacy Act*](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/p-21/).

[View Info Source](/cpc/en/our-company/transparency-and-trust/privacy-centre/info-source.page)

## Requesting information

![](/cpc/assets/cpc/img/icons/request.svg)

### Requests under the *Access to Information Act*

The [*Access to Information Act*](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/A-1) (ATIA) allows access to Canada Post records for:

* Canadian citizens
* Other people who are present in Canada
* Corporations that are present in Canada

Get a copy of past records released under the *Access to Information Act* or view summaries of the monthly records.

[See completed access to information requests](/cpc/en/our-company/transparency-and-trust/access-to-information/completed-access-to-information-requests.page)

![](/cpc/assets/cpc/img/icons/legal-name.svg)

### How to make an Access to Information request

1. **Complete the Access to Information Request Form**

   [Download and complete the Access to Information Request form](/cpc/doc/en/aboutus/accesstoinformation.pdf).
2. **Include your application fee**

   Each request must include a $5 application fee by cheque or money order payable to Canada Post Corporation.
3. **Mail your form and application fee to:**

   Access to Information Directorate
   Canada Post Corporation
   2701 Riverside Drive Suite N1160
   OTTAWA ON K1A 0B1

   or

   **Email your form to:**
   ATIP\_AIPRP@canadapost.postescanada.ca

**Note:** You can also submit requests online on the [Government of Canada website](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/access-information-privacy/access-information/request-information.html).

![](/cpc/assets/cpc/img/icons/file-a-claim.svg)

### Requests under the *Privacy Act*

Requests under the [*Privacy Act*](https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/ENG/ACTS/P-21/index.html) may be submitted by:

* Canadian citizens
* Other people who are present in Canada

The request must be made by the person to whom the information relates or their duly authorized representative.

![](/cpc/assets/cpc/img/icons/checklist.svg)

### How to make a *Privacy Act* request

1. **Complete the Personal Information Request Form**

   [Download and complete the Personal Information Request form](https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/tbsf-fsct/350-58-nf-eng.pdf).
2. **Mail your form to:**

   Access to Information Directorate
   Canada Post Corporation
   2701 Riverside Drive Suite N1160
   OTTAWA ON K1A 0B1

   or

   **Email your form to:**
   ATIP\_AIPRP@canadapost.postescanada.ca

**Note:** You can also submit requests online on the [Government of Canada website](https://www.canada.ca/en/treasury-board-secretariat/services/access-information-privacy/access-information/request-information.html).
